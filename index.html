Barcamp Southampton in 3 easy steps…

The concept is beautiful in it's simplicity: We hand the power of the conference over to our audience, and see what they come up with. That's it. Free from the shackles of a more traditional event, you will have the chance to learn completely unexpected things, and will also be able to share your knowledge with like-minded others. And have fun. That too.

1

Powered by the people

All the speakers will be attendees, and regular people just like you! In fact, every attendee is encouraged to participate in some way - You could run a session, give a presentation, or help with someone elses, for example.

2

Structure, but not as you know it

We collaboratively build a schedule in the morning, with willing volunteers selecting time slots in the available rooms. We ensure the venue has the necessary power and presentation equipment, so all you have to do is turn up and be awesome.

3

For good (not evil)

We're in this for the community, not for gain or profit. To that end, Barcamp Southampton is totally free to attend. We don't care about what's in your wallet, we care about what you have to offer and what you could learn.

Sound good? We think so.
